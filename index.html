About Me

Hi, I'm Chase. I am a full-stack software developer and problem solver. After serving almost five years in the military, I knew that I wanted a career that was going to challenge my mind. I've been intrigued by computers ever since my parents brought one home in the 90s. So As my enlistment started to come to an end I decided to look into programming, and I was hooked. I spent a year and a half at Kennesaw State University pursuing a Computer Science Degree, but ultimately was unsatisfied with the education I was receiving. I did some research and decided to attend Nashville Software School's full-time web development bootcamp, and it was a great decision as they gave me the skills needed to hit the ground running. Following graduation, I was offered a teaching assistant contract with the school. It was very rewarding to help guide a cohort through a three month Python / Django back-end course, many of whom are working as software developers now. I then worked a short software development contract with a start-up in California which was mainly focused on web scraping. I believe my experience has helped me become a well rounded person, and I am eager to help businesses succeed through quality software.

Projects

SupplyMate
SupplyMate is an inventory and logistics management system. It was designed for any company that maintains an inventory and has remote employees that pull from that inventory regularly. This was built with a React front-end and a Django REST Framework API with a SQLite database.
github.com/chase-fite/supplymate-client github.com/chase-fite/supplymate-api
Level Up
Level Up is a workout organizer and data visualization tool. A user can create their own exercises and workouts in a variety of formats. It was designed as a responsive web app so users could easily access their workout plans from their phone. A user can also record their workout results and view graphs of their performance over a selected time period. This app was built with React and uses JSON-Server as a pseudo REST API to persist data.
github.com/chase-fite/level-up
